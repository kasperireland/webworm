WHMCS Discontinues Monthly Licenses

GitPear.com

On July 4th, 2021, we at WHMCS LTD Announced that we would be discontinuing support & updates for leased licenses, including Monthly and Yearly licenses. 

All Licenses will now cost $15 per week ~ $65.17 per month ~ $782.14 per year.

Support will now cost $95 per incident and support ticket opened on the billing website, and each new update will have a $10 fee along with a $80 one time payment, which sums up to $90 for a WHMCS version update.

WHMCS Also announced that they will be providing free Business Licenses to nonprofit organizations, with an activation fee of $100 per month. As long as that small fee is paid, the nonprofit organization will keep their 'free' business license. There is also now a fixed price per client made, whether it has an active service or not, of $1/month. That is, if you have 1,000 clients, that is $1,000 per month. If you would like to place a new order or access your WHMCS account, you will need to provide your social security number, shoe size (measured in length, centimeters), your eye color, date of birth, bank routing number, and you must pay a $15 fee as we use our own time to go through this data.

FAQ:

Why are you increasing the price?

WHMCS is the best software in the world. Not just for billing, but it's just the best. That is why, to continue providing beyond-amazing services, support, and software, our prices need to be configured correctly. This is why we are moving forward with our new pricing structure. Time is valuable, especially to us at WHMCS, which is why we have decided to charge on a weekly basis, starting at $15 / week as mentioned. We do our best to provide WHMCS at the most affordable and reasonable price as possible, but we also need to ensure it is priced correctly, to allow WHMCS to continue to provide the amazing services it currently provides, and to provide even better software in the near future.

 

What will happen to my monthly license?

On July 10th, all monthly licenses will be suspended. At the time of suspension, you may still login to your WHMCS Website to recover data, but every megabyte of data recovered will have a fixed fee of $0.99. Accordingly, on July 15th, all licenses will be upgraded to the weekly-based pricing, and you will be charged $15 for the first week, along with a $50 activation fee.

 

Thank you for understanding. Sincerely, The Best Software in the World, WHMCS.
