WebPros completes acquisition of Blesta

CEO OF EIG

WebPros entered a definitive agreement with Blesta in which WebPros would acquire Blesta for the price of $10 Billion. Paul from Blesta promised that every user in the Blesta Discord Community would receive $1 Million from him.

Since the acquisition of Blesta was completed, by WebPros, Blesta Owned Licenses were discontinued and Monthly Licenses were bumped up in price by 250%, each having active client limits of at least 250.

Blesta is now offering a migration tool, to migrate from Blesta to WHMCS, as Blesta will be getting merged with WHMCS in November. For every 10 seconds spent using this migration tool, a $5 invoice will be created. That is, if you spend 250 seconds using the migration tool, you would owe $1,250 to WebPros. WebPros was recently asked why there was a fee for such a thing, here is what they had to say:

We at WebPros only make quality software. This is why, for users which are using our beautiful migration tool, an invoice will be created, as you are using our precious well-developed high quality industry-leading feature-rich modern cross-compatible software for your own use. Failure to pay this invoice within 2 minutes will result in a 50% increase of the invoice price, because you chose to ignore a very clear notification stating you had an overdue invoice.
